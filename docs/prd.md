1. Executive Summary
Nama Wellness is a web-based wellness and learning marketplace that connects students, teachers, and organizations through live and recorded learning experiences.
The platform supports wellness-focused and skill-based education including:
Yoga
Meditation
Music
Vocal Training
Instrument Training
Arts & Crafts
Creative Learning
Future categories managed by administrators
The platform consists of two primary business offerings:
EdPro
A B2C learning marketplace where students enroll in courses offered by approved teachers.
Corporate
A B2B wellness solution allowing organizations to provide wellness and learning programs to employees.

2. Product Vision
To become the leading wellness and skill-learning ecosystem that empowers teachers to teach, students to learn, and organizations to improve employee well-being through structured digital experiences.
